# SHACK-SERVER Manifesto

## Why

Amateur radio generates an incredible amount of information.

DX spots.

Solar data.

Propagation.

Station telemetry.

Cluster messages.

Contest data.

Unfortunately, most software simply displays this information.

Operators are left to interpret everything themselves.

SHACK-SERVER follows a different philosophy.

---

## Our Mission

Turning amateur radio data into actionable information.

---

## Our Vision

Build the Operations Center for modern amateur radio.

A place where information becomes understanding.

Understanding becomes decisions.

And decisions become better operating.

---

## Our Principles

Information before decoration.

Decisions before data.

Quality before speed.

Consistency before complexity.

Documentation before implementation.

Architecture before features.

---

## Our Goal

Every operator should know within seconds:

• What is happening.

• Why it matters.

• What to do next.

---

## Built for the Future

SHACK-SERVER is designed as a platform.

Not as a single application.

Every module should work together.

Every feature should support the operator.

Every decision should make operating easier.

---

## Made in Switzerland 🇨🇭

Designed and developed with passion for Amateur Radio.

Turning amateur radio data into actionable information.
